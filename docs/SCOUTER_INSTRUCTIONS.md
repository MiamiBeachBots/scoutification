#  Scouter Instructions

You are scouting a match. At the end of each match, you'll generate a QR code that the middleman laptop will scan to send your data to the server.

**If you have WiFi**, you can skip the QR step entirely and submit directly.

---

## Option A — QR Code Mode (works without WiFi)

### Step 1 — Open the scouting form
Go to: **`https://scout.thaliathenerd.dev`**

> Bookmark this! You'll open it many times.

### Step 2 — Fill out the form

Fill in all the fields as the match happens:
- **Team Number** and **Match Number** — fill these in before the match starts
- **Scouter Name** — your name (only needs to be done once per session)
- Use the **+/−** buttons to count scored balls, penalties, etc.
- Checkboxes for taxi and robot breakdown
- Dropdowns for climb, defense, driver skill

> Your form data is **automatically saved** in your phone's browser — if you close the tab by accident, your draft is still there when you reopen it.

### Step 3 — Generate the QR code

Tap **"Generate QR Code"** at the bottom of the form.

A large QR code will appear on screen.

### Step 4 — Get it scanned

Hold your phone up toward the **middleman's scanner laptop**.

- You'll see the scanner flash green when it reads your code
- The middleman's screen will show your match and team number

### Step 5 — Start the next entry

Tap **"New Entry"** to reset the form for the next match.

---

## Option B — Direct Submit Mode (WiFi required)

If you have a working WiFi connection to the internet, you can skip the QR code step:

Go to: **`https://submit.scout.thaliathenerd.dev`**

Fill out the same form, then tap **"Submit to Server"**. You'll see a green  confirmation. Done — no QR code, no middleman needed.

---

## Tips

- **Turn up your screen brightness** before showing the QR code — low brightness makes it hard to scan
- **Don't leave the form page** mid-match or you'll lose your draft (unless you already saved it)
- If the form looks wrong or something is missing, tell your lead scout
- The history at the bottom of the QR code page shows your last 10 entries so you can verify what was submitted
